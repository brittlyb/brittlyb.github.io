1900-2021
Medical Blood Pump

This was invented in 1855, by Porter and Bradley. The blood pump, pumps blood through a system of blood vessels as the heart beats.


Anesthesia

This was invented in 1841, by Dr. Crawford W. Long. This is used to put patients asleep while doing surgery.


Stethoscope

This was invented in 1815, by René Laënnec. This is used to hear the heartbeat of a patient.


CT Scanner

This was invented in 1971, by Dr. Godfrey Hounsfield. This is used to scan the entire body to diagnose you with a disease, or injury. Also used to plan medical, surgical, or radiation treatment.


DNA Sequencing

This was invented in 1992, by Dr. Leroy E. Hood. This is used for finding someone's DNA.


Telemedicine

This was invented in the late 1960s, but it became a form of healthcare delivery in 2015.
